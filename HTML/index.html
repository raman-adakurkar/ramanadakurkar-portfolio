MY JOURNEY
I've always been fascinated by how things work. So, when smartphones first came out, I was hooked. I loved customizing every aspect of my phone, from the look to how it felt. I spent countless hours exploring different custom ROMs, trying to create the perfect phone experience.

But it wasn't enough. I wanted to build my own world, not just tweak someone else's. That’s when I decided to study Computer Science and Engineering. While learning was exciting, I quickly realized that textbooks couldn't teach me how to create the amazing designs I had in my head.

So, I took matters into my own hands and started learning about UI/UX design. I loved bringing my ideas to life on the screen. But deep down, I still wanted to build entire apps and systems.

To make that happen, I dived into programming, learning languages like C and C++. It was tough, but I was determined. Finally, I graduated and joined Coditas Technologies, Pune. There, I discovered the world of Full-Stack Development. It was like finding the missing piece to my puzzle.

Now, I get to combine my love for design with my passion for coding. I create amazing digital experiences from start to finish. It's been an incredible journey, and I can't wait to see what the future holds.
